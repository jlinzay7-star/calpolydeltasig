Our Foundation

What We Stand For

Founded in 1981, the Kappa Mu Chapter of Delta Sigma Pi is the foremost professional business fraternity on Cal Poly's campus. We develop and shape our brothers through our three pillars.

Professionalism

Being a professional business fraternity, we pride ourselves on providing learn by doing experiences through professional and leadership opportunities. While we are a fraternity only open to students of the Orfalea College of Business, our brothers are interested in a wide variety of companies, positions and industries.

Community Service

Our brothers participate in various service events, utilizing our creativity and business skills to build the surrounding community and give back to others. The brothers of Kappa Mu are dedicated to making a lasting impact in San Luis Obispo County by volunteering in various ways including marathons, fundraisers and food drives.

Brotherhood

Our chapter has a unique and inclusive culture unmatched by any other fraternity, sorority, or organization. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment, providing each and every brother the opportunity to feel connected with each other and to find their place within the chapter and university.

From Our Leader

Message From The President

Charlie Boarman

Chapter President

boarmancharlie@gmail.com

Founded on November 7, 1907, Delta Sigma Pi is a co-ed professional business fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities. With over 300,000 members, we are one of the most prominent business fraternities across the nation.

The Kappa Mu Chapter of California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo (SLO) was established on April 4, 1981. The Kappa Mu Chapter emphasizes its three pillars of professionalism, community service, and brotherhood.

The Kappa Mu chapter of Delta Sigma Pi provides professional opportunities through corporate tours, guest speakers, and our vast alumni network. With over 1,000 initiates, the Kappa Mu chapter has the resources to connect with brothers all over the world in a diverse set of industries.

This chapter capitalizes on giving back to the San Luis Obispo community to show our support and gratitude for local charities and organizations in the area. Delta Sigma Pi goes beyond professional and service aspects by connecting through brotherhood. It is through brotherhood that this chapter has bonded together to support each other at all times, and create memories that will last a lifetime. This chapter is a family not just for four years, but for life.

Please explore our website to learn more about Delta Sigma Pi, and do not hesitate to reach out to me to connect and learn more about the chapter.

Best, Charlie Boarman

Our Story

The History of Delta Sigma Pi

Delta Sigma Pi is a co-ed professional business fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities and was founded on November 7, 1907 at New York University by Harold Valentine Jacobs, Alexander Frank Makay, Alfred Moysello and Henry Albert Tienken.

With over 280,000 members and 200 collegiate chapters around the country, Delta Sigma Pi continues to develop principled business leaders for the future by providing a lifetime of opportunity for our members.
